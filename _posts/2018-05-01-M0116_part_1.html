I have a few old apple keyboards

Reverese engineering the matrix

This was not so hard, though it was quite tedious, Just a matter of finding which pins on the controller each side of each switch is connected to.

The matrix on the M0116 has 10 rows and 8 columns for 74 of the 81 switches, with the modifiers (and power key) connected to separate pins and the ground plane, the two shift keys are connected in parallel

Wiring it up with a teensy

The easiest way to wire this up without modifying the board is to copy the wiring and connect the 8x10 matrix
